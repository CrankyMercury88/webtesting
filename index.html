OUR

Thesis

Everyone can live 100 healthy, happy years...

Not through better pills or smarter surgeries, but by fundamentally rethinking how and when disease is detected.

The root cause of most disease is chronic inflammation. Chronic Inflammation is a vicious cycle that begins as a symptom, then turns into the primary driver of more than 200 diseases. It causes more than 30 million deaths globally each year and affects 2-3 billion people worldwide. It's the common thread connecting autoimmune disease, heart attacks, Alzheimer's, depression, diabetes, and cancer.

By monitoring inflammation, we offer true preventative healthcare. Our platform delivers lab-grade results from a small blood sample (10μL) in under 10 minutes, for under $10. It requires a simple fingerstick, with no prep or training needed so that anyone can do it. When blood testing is this fast and affordable it changes everything; you can do it weekly or daily, from your home, instead of once a year.

Frequent testing unlocks the true potential of AI in healthcare. You can't train predictive models or establish personal baselines with one or two tests per year; you need weekly or daily data. As we collect thousands of data points across millions of users, our AI learns to predict flares, optimize interventions, and spot patterns invisible to human clinicians. Every test makes the platform smarter. Every user generates insights that benefit the product and other users.

We're not just making testing faster and cheaper: we're creating the data infrastructure that shifts healthcare from reactive legacy systems to proactive and personalized care.

This is the moment when proactive healthcare replaces legacy systems.

OUR

Hardware

Q-SENS is a silicon photonic biosensor that measures cytokines from a single drop of blood.

Cytokines are the control signals for inflammation, immune response, and cell communication in the body. Tracking cytokine levels reveals inflammation before symptoms appear, enabling early intervention that can prevent disease progression across hundreds of conditions.

Our patented technology addresses the core bottlenecks:

  • Speed: Results in under 10 minutes, not days
  • Cost: Under $10 per test, not $200-500+
  • Ease: Simple fingerstick, no prep or training
  • Accuracy: Lab-grade precision (97.5% equivalency)
  • Scale: Designed for millions of tests per day

What makes Q-SENS different: Most biosensors struggle with sensitivity, cost, or complexity. Q-SENS uses silicon photonics to achieve lab-grade results at consumer scale. The platform is built on proven semiconductor manufacturing techniques, which means we can scale production rapidly once demand materializes.

We have 22 patents protecting the core technology, a working prototype validated through BARDA funding, and FDA 510(k) clearance targeted for Q4 2027.

OUR

Software

CORE AI turns raw cytokine data into actionable health insights.

The true value of Q-SENS isn't just measurement—it's interpretation. Our AI platform analyzes cytokine patterns to predict disease flares, optimize interventions, and personalize care at a level impossible with annual testing.

What CORE AI does:

  • Predictive analytics: Detects disease progression days to weeks before symptoms
  • Personalized baselines: Learns your unique inflammation signature over time
  • Intervention optimization: Recommends lifestyle, medication, or care adjustments
  • Pattern recognition: Identifies correlations across millions of users

The data advantage: Frequent testing generates exponentially more data than annual labs. This enables machine learning models that improve with every test, creating a network effect where each user makes the platform better for everyone.

Built on HIPAA-compliant infrastructure with end-to-end encryption, CORE AI is designed for both consumer wellness and clinical integration.
